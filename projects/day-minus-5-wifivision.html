The Problem

Traditional presence detection has major limitations:

  • Cameras invade privacy and feel intrusive
  • PIR sensors only detect motion, not presence
  • Commercial systems cost $200+ and require subscriptions
  • Most solutions fail in darkness or low visibility
  • Complex installation requiring professional setup

The Solution

WiFiVision uses WiFi signals to detect presence:

  • 100% privacy - no cameras, no video recording
  • Works in complete darkness - uses radio waves
  • Only $10-15 in hardware (ESP32 + cable)
  • ~100 Hz real-time detection sampling rate
  • Self-calibrating threshold system

How It Works

WiFi CSI Extraction

ESP32 captures Channel State Information from 52 subcarriers, measuring amplitude and phase changes in WiFi signals.

Signal Analysis

Real-time variance analysis detects when human bodies disrupt the WiFi field - like ripples in water when someone enters.

Visual Dashboard

4-panel matplotlib interface shows real-time CSI data, variance levels, detection status, and animated presence indicator.

Auto-Calibration

Self-calibrating threshold system adapts to your environment. No manual tuning required after initial setup.

100% Privacy

No cameras, no video, no images. Only radio wave analysis. Perfect for bedrooms, bathrooms, or anywhere privacy matters.

Works in Darkness

Uses 2.4GHz radio waves, not light. Detects presence whether lights are on, off, or in complete blackout conditions.
