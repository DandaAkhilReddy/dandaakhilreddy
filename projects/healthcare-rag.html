The Challenge

Healthcare AI presents unique and critical challenges:

  • Doctors make 50K+ clinical decisions daily that need AI support
  • Medical literature is vast and constantly updating
  • EHR data is unstructured and hard to query effectively
  • HIPAA compliance is mandatory - no room for errors
  • Accuracy is life-or-death in healthcare contexts

The Solution

A production RAG system built for healthcare:

  • 40% improved relevance over baseline retrieval methods
  • Processing 100GB+ healthcare data daily
  • Supporting 50K+ clinical decisions across 15+ hospitals
  • HIPAA compliant with enterprise security
  • GPT-4 powered with medical domain grounding
