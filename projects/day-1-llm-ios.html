The Problem

Using cloud-based LLMs comes with significant drawbacks:

  • Requires constant internet connection
  • API costs add up quickly ($20-100+/month)
  • Your private data sent to third-party servers
  • Latency issues with remote servers
  • Service outages and rate limits

The Solution

Run LLMs entirely on your iPhone:

  • 87 tokens/second - faster than most cloud APIs
  • Works completely offline - airplane mode friendly
  • 100% privacy - data never leaves your device
  • Zero ongoing costs - pay nothing after setup
  • 12ms latency - instant responses

Quick Start Guide

1

Download the Model

Get Qwen3-0.6B in GGUF format from Hugging Face (~400MB):

https://huggingface.co/Qwen/Qwen3-0.6B-GGUF

Choose the Q4_K_M quantization for best speed/quality balance.

2

Install PocketPal AI

Download the free app from the App Store:

Search "PocketPal AI" on App Store
3

Transfer via iCloud

Move the .gguf file to your iPhone:

iCloud Drive > PocketPal AI > Models
4

Load and Chat!

Open PocketPal AI, select your model, and start chatting. Enable reasoning mode for complex tasks.

Settings > Enable "Thinking Mode" for reasoning
